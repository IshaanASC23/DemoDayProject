Our Vision

We are very passionate about people's access to education. Using our new knowledge of code, us scholars developed this website to help raise awareness and advocate for the education for our young minds of the future.


This site was created to specifically help kids around kindergarten to 1st grade who are unable to access good education. We provide our young users with basic mathematical understanding of addition and subtraction.

We encourage parents to visit our about page to learn more about our processes in creating this platform, as well as to view links that can further support their childrens learning.
